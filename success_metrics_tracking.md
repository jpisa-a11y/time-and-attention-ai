### Weekly Optimization Process

**Monday - Review:**
- Analyze past week's performance
- Compare to targets
- Identify top/bottom performers
- Note audience feedback

**Tuesday - Analyze:**
- Deep dive into top content
- Understand what resonated
- Identify patterns
- Extract learnings

**Wednesday - Plan:**
- Plan next week's content
- Incorporate learnings
- Adjust strategy if needed
- Schedule posts

**Thursday - Optimize:**
- A/B test new approaches
- Refine messaging
- Improve visuals
- Enhance CTAs

**Friday - Execute:**
- Launch optimized content
- Monitor performance
- Engage with audience
- Prepare for next week

### Monthly Optimization Cycle

**Week 1:** Analyze previous month's performance
**Week 2:** Identify optimization opportunities
**Week 3:** Implement changes and test
